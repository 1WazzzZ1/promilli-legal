Privacy Policy for PROMILLI

Last updated: April 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how PROMILLI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our app. By using PROMILLI, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Definitions

App: The PROMILLI mobile application.

User: Any individual who accesses or uses the App.

Personal Data: Any data that relates to an identified or identifiable person (e.g. name, profile picture, session data).

Sessions: Shared experiences inside the app where users track drinks and estimated BAC with friends.

BAC: Blood Alcohol Content calculated using the Widmark formula based on user input.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data when you use the App:

  • Name and profile picture
  • Session data and drink history
  • Chat messages (temporarily stored for session communication and automatically deleted after 6 hours)
  • App usage statistics (e.g. drinks added, BAC trends)
  • Technical data such as device type, IP address, and browser headers

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Calculate and display BAC estimates
  • Store and show your drink history
  • Enable real-time social features such as sessions and chat
  • Personalize your profile and statistics
  • Send optional notifications or updates (e.g. BAC updates)
  • Improve performance, security, and user experience

4. Data Sharing

We may share your data in the following cases:

  • With friends in your sessions: Your BAC, drinks, and messages are visible to session participants.
  • With service providers: For backend hosting, analytics, and storage (e.g. Supabase, Cloudflare R2).
  • With legal authorities: If required by Danish law or court order.
  • With your consent: If you explicitly agree to external sharing.

5. Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure (e.g. Supabase, Cloudflare R2). Data is retained only as long as necessary for functionality, legal compliance, or user history.

6. User Control and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using the in-app "Delete Account" feature.
